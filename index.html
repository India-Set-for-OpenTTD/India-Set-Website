Welcome!

India Set for OpenTTD is a planned set of newgrfs that aim to represent India as much as possible.


Planned newgrfs under this project include the following:


  • India Set:Trains: Railway rolling stock used by Indian Railways and it's predecessors in pre independence India. Might be subdivided into separate newgrfs if needed.

  • India Set:Stations:Stations used by Indian Railways and it's predecessors . Will be divided into three categories: freight stations,iconic stations and generic stations.

  • India Set:Buildings: Buildings found in India . Will contain generic buildings as well as monuments (eventually).

  • India Set:Objects: Object sets to supplement the overall aesthetic to the different parts of this projects.


For more details regarding individual projects, kindly refer to the individual pages under the Projects tab.
