India Set: Stations

India Set:Stations is intended to complement India Set:Trains mainly and the rest of the set.

The proposed set of stations will be divided into three separate categories:

  • Freight stations:As the name suggests , it will contain stations and other effects pertaining to freight.
  • Iconic stations: This will contain stations of historical and national significance, such as Mumbai's Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus(aka Victoria Terminus) , Chennai's Chennai Central, Kolkata's Howrah station , to name a few.
  • Generic stations:As the name suggests,it will contain generic stations and station buildings.
